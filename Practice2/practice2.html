Furniture

Furniture consists of large objects such as tables, chairs, or beds that are used in a room for sitting or lying on or for putting things on or in. Each piece of furniture in their home suited the style of the house.


Home Decor

Home Décor short for home decoration, interior décor encompasses items and you guessed it, decorations, that make your home look nice. This can include a range of items such as furniture, art, plants, and accessories.
